There are a few ways to level up. The most common is to go on raid, go back to base and level up based on the result of the raid. 

XP is gained predominantly by going on raids, as this is the best way for a gobbo to show that he is boss material. XP is based both on the action during the raid, as well as any loot brought back home. 

Another way to level up is to die! Because when a goblin dies, there always an ambitious bastard of a goblin ready to take its place. And as this goblin has survived whiles you have not, it probably is ready to step into your shoes. So it will not start from the beginning but possibly even further up than you in abilities.
