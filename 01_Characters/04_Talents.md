# Talents & Achievements

## What are Talents?
While stats dictate your raw dice pool, and equipment dictates your loadout, **Talents** are your dirty tricks. They are the rule-breakers that let you ignore standard combat penalties, gain free actions, or stack highly situational Boons. 

## Unlocking & Buying Talents
Goblins don't learn by sitting in a classroom; they learn by surviving incredibly stupid decisions. Because of this, you cannot simply buy a Talent out of a book when you level up. You have to earn the right to buy it through blood, sweat, and fire. 

### 1. The Deed (Unlocking)
Every Talent is locked behind a specific, often dangerous, in-game achievement called a **Deed**. A PC must complete this Deed during a raid.
*If you complete the Deed but die on that same raid, the Talent is NOT unlocked! You have to survive the raid to pass the knowledge on.*

### 2. The Purchase
Once a Talent is unlocked, the PC may spend their hard-earned XP to purchase and equip it instead of increasing a Stat.

### 3. Gang Inheritance (The Easy Way)
When a PC retires and becomes an **Elder**, or dies a glorious death and joins the **Pile of Legends**, any Talents they had purchased become permanently unlocked for their entire Gang. 
*   **The Benefit:** A new PC from that Gang does not have to perform the dangerous Deed again! They can simply spend their starting XP (gained from Gang Renown) to buy the inherited Talent before their very first raid. 
*   *This encourages long-running Gangs to build "Skill Trees" naturally over multiple generations of Gobbos.*

## Example Talent List & Deeds

### Survival & Toughness
*   **Iron Stomach:** You can eat literal garbage or poison to heal 1 wound (Roll a Tough test to see if you get sick instead).
    *   *The Deed:* Survive being Poisoned and dropping to 1 Health.
*   **Fire-Dancer:** Ignore the first source of Fire damage per raid.
    *   *The Deed:* End a combat encounter while actively on fire.
*   **Hard Head:** Ignore the first Normal wound you take.
    *   *The Deed:* Take falling damage and survive.
*   **Pack Mule:** Ignore the "hard test" penalty when dragging heavy Loot (Movement is still halved).
    *   *The Deed:* Successfully drag a Bulk 4 (or higher) item entirely out of a dungeon while under attack.

### Combat & Cruelty
*   **Butcher:** +1d to Violence against enemies smaller than you.
    *   *The Deed:* Kill an enemy without rolling any 1s on your attack.
*   **Ankle-Biter:** Whenever you successfully Dodge an attack, you get a free Reaction Slink attack against the attacker's legs.
    *   *The Deed:* Successfully Dodge an attack from an enemy that is at least Size 3 larger than you.
*   **Slippery:** Enemies do not get free reactionary attacks against you when you leave their Zone.
    *   *The Deed:* Spend an entire combat encounter doing nothing but running away and dodging.

### Leadership & Mobs
*   **Despot:** +1 Max Grunt.
    *   *The Deed:* Defeat an enemy completely by yourself without any allied Mobs in your zone assisting you.
*   **Meat Shield:** Once per round, allocate damage meant for you to an adjacent allied Mob.
    *   *The Deed:* Kill at least one goblin in your own Mob to regain Grunt during combat.
*   **Inspiring Death:** Regain 1 Grunt when one of your own Mobs is entirely wiped out in your Zone (showing them what happens when they fail you).
    *   *The Deed:* Survive a raid where every single Mob you commanded was killed.
*   **Rat-Whisperer:** You can use Mouth instead of Brains to Notice things, assuming there are vermin around to ask.
    *   *The Deed:* Tame, capture, or befriend a local dungeon pest.
