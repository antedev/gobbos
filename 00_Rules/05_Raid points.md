# Raid Points 
Raid points are a way to measure the success of a raid. They are collected when the PC goes out on a raid, and are then converted back into XP, when they return back to the lair, and brag about their success to the other goblins. Typically raid points are awarded for destroying stuff, taking control of key locations or in other way doing something in a particular Raid location. 

## Raid Locations
A Raid location can by anything from a small camp or a dungeon, to a dwarven mine or dragons lair.
