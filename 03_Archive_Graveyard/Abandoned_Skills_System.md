# Abandoned Skills System

*Archived from 00_Stats.md. Kept here in case we want to revisit a more granular skill system, but currently removed to keep character creation and resolution fast and chaotic.*

## Skills
There are 8 skills in the game, two for each Main stat, with a bonus for Brains should your PC have been able to wield Magic for some reason. 
### Strenght
#### Violence
Attacking something that is close by. 
#### Lift 
Lifting heavy stuff, or other acts of strength 
### Slink
#### Sneak
Try to not be seen
#### Dodge
Try to not be hit

### Brains
#### Tinker
Manipulate things 
#### Notice
See if there is something screwy going on

#### Mouth
#### Order 
Order other goblins around
#### Lying 
Lying about things
