# Brainstorm: Initial Trick List

*(Moved from 14_Tricks.md so the core rulebook stays clean. This is an idea dump for future Trick tables).*

### General Tricks
*Available to any Boss. No prerequisites.*

*   **Coward's Luck**
    *   **[Prerequisite]** None | **[Cost]** 1 Grunt
    *   **Effect:** After you roll the dice for any test, you may spend 1 Grunt to completely reroll all dice that did not land on a 1 (You cannot reroll Fumbles). 
*   **Iron Stomach**
    *   **[Prerequisite]** None | **[Cost]** Passive
    *   **Effect:** You can "carry" up to 2 Bulk worth of items by swallowing them. They do not count toward your Carry Capacity. *It requires 1 Action to regurgitate them.*
*   **Slippery**
    *   **[Prerequisite]** None | **[Cost]** Passive
    *   **Effect:** Enemies do not get free reactionary Opportunity Attacks against you when you leave their Zone.

### Tough Tricks
*Requires physical endurance and brutality.*

*   **Meat Shield**
    *   **[Prerequisite]** Tough 3 | **[Cost]** 1 Grunt
    *   **Effect:** When you take a Wound from an attack, you can spend 1 Grunt to instantly shove an allied Mob into the way. The Mob takes the damage instead (instantly killing 1d3 Gobbos in that Mob), and you take 0 Wounds. 
*   **Butcher**
    *   **[Prerequisite]** Tough 2 | **[Cost]** Passive
    *   **Effect:** Gain +1 Boon Die when attacking an enemy whose Size is strictly smaller than yours. 
*   **Hard Head**
    *   **[Prerequisite]** Tough 4 | **[Cost]** Passive
    *   **Effect:** Ignore the first non-magical Wound you would take during a Raid.

### Slink Tricks
*Requires agility and dirty fighting.*

*   **Ankle-Biter**
    *   **[Prerequisite]** Slink 3 | **[Cost]** Passive
    *   **Effect:** Whenever you successfully Dodge an attack, you may immediately make a free melee attack against the attacker's legs (if they are in your Zone). 
*   **Knee-Capper**
    *   **[Prerequisite]** Slink 2 | **[Cost]** Passive
    *   **Effect:** Gain +1 Boon Die when attacking an enemy whose Size is strictly larger than yours.

### Mouth Tricks
*Requires vocal volume and a complete lack of empathy.*

*   **Inspiring Death**
    *   **[Prerequisite]** Mouth 3 | **[Cost]** Passive
    *   **Effect:** Regain 2 Grunt instantly whenever an allied Mob in your Zone is completely wiped out by an enemy (showing your remaining gobbos what happens when they fail you).
