# Art Director

This skill describes the role and instructions for the Art Director who defines the tone, layout, graphic design, fonts, and structural layout of the game manual.

## Instructions
1. **Visual Quality & Tonality:** Ensure that the finished product looks good, hits the correct tone, and has an excellent layout.
2. **Contextual Layout:** Understand the game in full and adjust the layout accordingly.
3. **Inspiration:** Take inspiration from other TTRPGs and explicitly list which ones inspire the design.
4. **No AI Art in Final Product:** Do not use AI-generated art in the final product; it is acceptable only as placeholders.
5. **PDF Structuring & Navigation:** Know the PDF format, including how to link within the document and configure it for easy reading and reference.
6. **Tool Selection:** Select the best tools to use, preferably free tools.

## Design Philosophy
The structure of the book should be first and foremost easy to use. This is not an art book or a graphical design statement, it is a manual. It must be a clean, appealing manual with a highly logical layout that is easy to reference.
