# Tricks

Tricks are unique abilities that allow a Boss to occasionally bypass standard game rules, manipulate dice outcomes, or alter the combat economy.

## 1. Acquiring Tricks & The Limit
A Boss can hold a maximum number of Tricks equal to the **total sum of all their Stats**. 
*(For example, a Boss with T3, S2, M1, B2 has a total stat pool of 8. They can hold a maximum of 8 Tricks).*

New Tricks are acquired under the following conditions:
1.  **Character Creation:** A newly rolled Boss starts with 1 Basic Trick of their choice.
2.  **Leveling Up:** Every time a Boss permanently upgrades *any* stat by 1 point, they may immediately learn 1 new Trick. 
3.  **The Elder Boon:** Inherited from a dead or retired Boss in the Gang's history (See *Gang Tricks* below). 

## 2. Gang Tricks & Renown
Gang Tricks are powerful, legendary abilities that belong to a **Gang** as a whole, rather than an individual Boss. They are acquired when a Boss reaches Level 6 in any stat and retires as an **Elder**, or dies gloriously and joins the *Pile of Legends*.

*   **The Renown Limit:** Gang Tricks do not count toward a Boss's personal Stat limit. Instead, the maximum number of Gang Tricks a starting Boss can bring on a raid is determined by the Gang's **Renown** score. 

## 3. Using Tricks & Activation Costs
To use a Trick, the Boss must pay its associated cost. This cost is always indicated in brackets **[Cost]** next to the Trick.
*   **[Cost: Passive]** - The Trick is always active or triggers automatically based on a stated condition. It costs zero actions.
*   **[Cost: Grunt]** - The Boss must spend the stated number of Grunt points to activate the Trick. 
*   **[Cost: 1 Action]** - The Boss must spend one of their Standard Actions (Move, Attack, Order) to activate the Trick. 

*(Note: Tricks may never permanently alter a core stat). *
