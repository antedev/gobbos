# Stats
The PC has a number of stats: 
## Main stats
Your main stats goes from 1-5, where 1 is the starting value, and 5 as high as you go. The number indicates the number of extra dice you will roll in any test related to that stat 
The main stats are: 
* Tough (T) - Used for violence, intimidation and lifting stuff
* Slink (S) - User for sneaking, dodging and all manners of dirty tricks
* Brains (B) - Used for notices stuff, tinkering, traps and for the odd case, magic
* Mouth (M) - Used for shouting orders, lying and trading 

## Secondary Stats
The secondary or derived stats are: 
* Grit - How tough you are, and how much damage you can take. Grit is 2xT
* Grunt - Your authority and energy - Used to command mobs, and bend goblins to your will. Grunt is equal to your second highest stat. 
* Movement - Is how far you can move in a turn. You start with a movement of 4, but it can be increased as you get higher stat in Slink. 
* Carry - Is how much Bulk you can carry. You start with a [[Carry]] of 6, and it is increased by 2 per Tough. (So if you have 2 Tough, you can carry 10 Bulk) See [[Loot]] for more information.

## Abilities 
#### Talents
Talents are what make your Gobbo unique from any other Gobbos, giving you an edge in specific circumstances. Typically you can gain talents as you play, but you also get some talents when you start, depending on the level you start at.
