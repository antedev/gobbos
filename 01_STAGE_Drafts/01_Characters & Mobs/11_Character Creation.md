# Character Creation 
-- 
Design goal: Fast, easy, but with some meaningful choices. Especially as we will create characters on higher levels. 
--

Creating a Character especially in lower levels should be fast, and can be completly random. However, as the game progresses, and new goblins step forward, the process will include more tactical choices. 

## Prime Goblin 
The first Goblin you create is a bit different from the following, as you not will just start out on your own, you are the boss of one of the [[Gangs]] in the [[Lair]], and you will start out creating you [[Gangs]] as well. 

### Create your Gang

#### Basics
Just the fluff and lore
* Name
* Icon and colors
* Slogan
* Qurik
#### Game mechanics
Things that actually have a game effect. Setting up the first member of the Legend Heap, the founding old boss, that will give some starting effects and that sets the direction from:
* **Old Boss (Founder)** - Your founding ancestor of the Gang. Grants 1 specific starting **Gang Trick** (See [[Tricks]]). 
* **Named item** - An old relic that showcase your right to rule the Gang.
* **Renown** - Your Gang's prestige and success over generations. **You start with Renown 1.** Your Gang gains +1 Renown whenever an active Boss dies gloriously and is added to the *Pile of Legends*, or when they hit Level 6 in any Stat and retire as an *Elder*. A Gang's Renown dictates how many **Gang Tricks** a newly rolled Boss in that Gang can bring on a raid!

### Create your Goblin
* Name - A good name is important, it will be used a lot and remembered forever. 
* Stats - Set up the stats
* Trick - One Trick that sets your goblin apart 
* Patreon - A spiritual leader, that grants some mechanical benefits, in the spirit of that Patreon. 
* Equipment - What really gives your goblin some configuration options.
