# Zones
In Gobbos Zones are used to create a framework for movement, especially in combat. A zone can be of any type of shape, but is often clearly defined by the environment, such as a room, a corridor or a field. Sometimes a larger are is split mulitpel zones, if the area is to large or several smaller areas close to each other are to be considered as one zone. 
A payers movement is measured in number of zones, and a normal movement speed is to move 2 zones per round and action. 
## Zone Attributes
A Zone zone can have a number of attributes tied to it, that describe the environment and conditions in the zone. Typically these has some effect on movement, but an also provide danger, such as traps, fire, water, etc. But it could also provide cover or give other positive effects for whoever is in control of it.
