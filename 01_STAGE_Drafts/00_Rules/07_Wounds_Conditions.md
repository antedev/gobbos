# Conditions
There are a number of conditions that can affect a gobbo. Most of these have a direct effect on one Stat for a PC, or a varity of effects for a mob.

* Weakend - -1d on Tough 
* Restrained - -1d on Slink, Movement becomes 0
* Confused - -1d on Brains
* Silenced - -1d on Mouth
* Blinded - -1 on Physical tests
* Deafened - -1d on Brain
* Poisoned - 
* Terrified - -1d Brain and Mouth tests, cannot move closer to the source of fear
* Clumsy - -1d on Slink and Tough tests
* Stunned - Cannot take any actions or reactions, automatically fails physical saves 
* Prone - -1d on all attacks, movement becomes 0
* Fatigued - -1d on all tests
* Exhausted - -2d on all tests
* Panicked -
