# Solar-Ichor Technology

*“You see, the tall-men think the canisters are magic flasks. They hold them with clean white gloves and pray to the sun. Gobbos know better. A canister is just a metal pipe stuffed with squeezed rock and hot earth-blood. If you insert the lead pin too far, the boiler runs hot. If you drop it on the floor, you run fast.”*
— *Snik-Griz, Scrap-Technician*

## The Raw Elements
Alchemical industrial technology in the Heartland relies on two primary physical components: Black Ichor and Sol-Quartz.

### Black Ichor
Black Ichor is a thick, volatile, metallic grease pumped from deep tectonic fissures in the earth.
*   **Physical Nature**: Cold, inert in the dark, and unburnable by normal fire. It carries a heavy stench of copper and ozone.
*   **Biological Toxicity**: Raw Ichor is a severe biohazard to humans. Prolonged exposure causes madness, black weeping tumors, and cellular rot. 
*   **The Raw Loop**: The natural, unrefined state of the material. Because Goblins are born from pools where this sludge accumulates, they possess a biological immunity to its toxic effects.

### Sol-Quartz
Sol-Quartz is a rare, flawless, milk-white crystal mined from deep geological veins.
*   **Physical Nature**: In its passive state, Sol-Quartz behaves like common glass. It is tough, non-combustible, and inert.
*   **Alchemical Nature**: The crystal lattice has extreme spiritual elasticity, acting as a molecular sponge that can absorb and store massive amounts of thermal and kinetic energy when under pressure.

## The Refining Process
Refineries are massive stone vaults constructed directly over open tectonic fissures. Concentrating light and heat is the key to cracking the fuel.

```
[ Concentrated Sunlight ] ──► Concentrates down Sun-Glass Spires
                                      │
                                      ▼
[ Black Ichor + Sol-Quartz ] ──► Pressurized in Refractory Vaults
                                      │
                                      ├─► Aurelium (Glowing Golden Liquid)
                                      └─► The Dross (Acidic Mutagenic Sludge)
```

1.  **Concentration**: Monumental Sun-Glass Mirror Spires on the surface capture raw sunlight, focusing it into blinding, high-temperature beams.
2.  **Solar Cracking**: The concentrated light is directed down through heavy quartz lenses into the subterranean vats filled with raw Black Ichor and Sol-Quartz.
3.  **Distillation**: Under extreme solar heat and alchemical pressure, the raw Ichor separates into two substances:
    *   **Aurelium**: A distilled, glowing golden liquid. It is odorless, smokeless, and hyper-dense, storing the energy of the sun in its molecular bonds.
    *   **The Dross**: A heavy, corrosive, tar-like black sludge left at the bottom of the vats. The Dross emits toxic, mutagenic fumes and dissolves stone and metal over time.

## Mechanics of the Canister
Refined Aurelium is packaged into standardized, heavy brass cylinders known as Stamped Vessels or alchemical canisters.

### The Catalytic Needle
A refined canister remains stable indefinitely at room temperature. To release the stored energy, you must introduce a catalyst.
*   **The Catalyst**: A needle or rod made of lead or cold iron is mechanically pressed into the core of the Aurelium.
*   **The Reaction**: The metal needle disrupts the molecular structure of the stabilized liquid, triggering a rapid discharge of intense, high-temperature infrared heat.
*   **The Throttle**: The reaction is entirely mechanical. Pushing the needle deeper increases the heat output. Pulling the needle out halts the reaction, allowing the canister to cool down.

>> **WARNING:** If a canister is run past its thermal limits, or if the containment brass suffers micro-fractures, the core will undergo sudden alchemical fission. The canister will instantly vaporize, destroying the machine and turning the surrounding zone to ash.

## The Grand Desynchronization
One hundred years ago, the alchemical network collapsed. To power the expanding colonies, the Empire pushed its refineries past their structural safety limits.

### Lattice Collapse
The system suffered a universal mechanical failure of the material:
*   **The Snapping**: A silent, planet-wide resonance wave snapped through the alchemical matrix.
*   **The Fracture**: Every Sol-Quartz crystal in existence fractured internally, forming billions of microscopic spiderweb cracks.
*   **The Leak**: Fractured crystals can no longer hold a stable charge. They leak energy constantly, becoming volatile, dangerous, and prone to rapid discharge.

### The Black Market Fuel
Because the imperial process is lost, modern factions must rely on bootleg refining methods:
*   **Bootleg Process**: Smugglers and Goblins refine raw Black Ichor using mismatched mirrors and unblessed, cracked quartz.
*   **The Result**: The fuel lacks the stabilizing additives of the old Empire. It is dirty, highly toxic, and volatile, but it still runs the engines.
*   **Oddity Properties**: High-tier engine parts salvaged from the ruins function as **Oddities**. Because they are powered by leaky, fractured crystals, they carry a high **Bite Level** (**B2** or **B3**), risking a catastrophic **Overclock** explosion when pushed in combat.
